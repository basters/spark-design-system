### Usage

Display company accolades and disclaimers on a page or in a [Footer](/using-spark/components/footer).

### Guidelines

- Heading text can wrap.
- Award images will stack by default and become side by side on larger viewports.
- Award images may be clickable links.
- Default to [Toggle](/using-spark/components/toggle) usage guidelines for the disclaimer.
